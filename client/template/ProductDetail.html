Product Description

From the popular smartphone game "GODDESS OF VICTORY: NIKKE" comes a 1/7 scale figure of Scarlet, a NIKKE from Pioneer. Be charmed by her playful smile peeking out from under her large tsunogasa hat. Scarlet's distinctive outfit and Fleetly Fading have been carefully sculpted, featuring detailed paintwork that is not to be missed. Interchangeable back hair parts are also included. Her hat can also be removed, allowing for different display options. Be sure to welcome the beautiful Scarlet into your collection.
